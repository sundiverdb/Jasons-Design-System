Tyrian

– HealthEquity's Design System

Tyrian purple is a natural dye produced by predatory sea snails, the dye is color fast and was extremely difficult to extract, textiles made with this dye became prized royal luxuries. It is believed that the intensity of the purple hue improved rather than faded as the dyed cloth aged.

Tyrian is constructed to help prototype and build out HealthEquity's applications and products. Built on top of Bootstrap, an open source toolkit for developing with HTML, CSS, and JS.
