Our Design System

Tyrian is organized into five main sections.

  • Getting Started: The place to go if you are just getting familiar with Tyrian
  • Foundation: UX styles and design tokens to be used to create consistent experiences within our products
  • Components: Collection of interface elements that can be reused across our designs
  • Patterns: How we are solving common design problems, and what to do in those certain scenarios
  • Mobile: Specific styles, components, and patterns unique to our mobile platforms

Tyrian – Figma Library

The design library is an evolving document that contains all of the design system's visual assets, including components, icons, colors, etc. The library should help you work faster, with confidence that your product or experience is well aligned with Tyrian. It utilizes components and typography styles, see the Figma file to review the organization of the library.

Reach out to Jason Grotegut if you need access to the Tyrian – Figma Library.

Why Figma?

Figma has become the industry-standard for interaction design and is recommended by the HQY UX team. Figma is relatively affordable to license, and offers a free account option for users. There are also plenty of tutorials available online.

Releases

The HealthEquity UX team will release updates to the Figma library as often as possible, to keep it up to date with any changes made to the design system.
